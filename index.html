To whom it may concern,

Welcome to my humble abode!
I've got a confession to make: Deep down, I always have a feeling that I love writing, though I used to pay no heed to the hunch. But, I think I need to "thank" this pandemic, being stuck at home led me back to one of my long-lost dreams aka building my own website! Ergo, this homepage is a tribute to my very first step towards achieving that dream. *patting myself on the back*

FYI, I am mainly talking about our dearest Bali here, so ahem... do not expect anything apart from Balinese. Why? Because I decided to move to this gorgeous island last year. I dedicated this Journal to getting to know every single thing about Bali.

I am aware that this is far from good. However, there is always a room for polishing things up and I promise myself I am not going to stop learning. Overall, thank you for coming here, intentionally or not. I really hope you enjoy your time here as I take you on my journeys!
